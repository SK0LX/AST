Overview

The Automatic Sniper Tool (AST) is designed to empower users to snipe newly launched tokens on the Solana blockchain with precision and speed. By leveraging an on-chain scanner and a machine learning (ML) model, AST identifies and filters tokens with positive expected value, enabling users to capitalize on early opportunities.

Core Objective

AST automates the process of sniping new tokens on Solana by scanning the blockchain in real-time and using an ML model to predict token potential, ensuring users can act swiftly and efficiently.

Architecture

  • Blockchain Scanner: A server-side script continuously scans the Solana blockchain via API requests to nodes, detecting new token launches.
  • ML Model: An integrated machine learning model filters tokens based on their potential for positive mathematical expectation, prioritizing high-value opportunities.
  • User Interfaces: Access AST through a web application, an Android app, or a Telegram Mini App, providing flexibility and convenience.

User Access & Workflow

  • Authorization: Users log in to access a dashboard displaying trade logs, wallet balances, and sniping activity.
  • Private Key Handling: Your private key is transmitted via a secure protocol, stored only in RAM during operation, and never persisted on disk.
  • Fund Management: AST does not custody your funds. Instead, you delegate token management rights to the tool while your assets remain in your wallet.

Security & Transparency

  • Open Source: AST's codebase is fully open source, allowing users to audit and verify its security.
  • Delegated Control: Fund management occurs through delegation, ensuring your assets never leave your wallet.
  • Secure Key Handling: Private keys are stored in RAM during use and are not saved, minimizing exposure risks.

Business Model

For the MVP, AST operates on a profit-sharing model or voluntary donations. A small commission may be applied on profits, ensuring sustainability while keeping the tool accessible.

Competitive Advantage

AST offers lightning-fast token filtering, processing each Solana block in just 0.4 seconds. This speed ensures you’re among the first buyers, maximizing your chances of securing high-potential tokens before others.
